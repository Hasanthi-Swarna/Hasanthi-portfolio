Local Services Discovery Platform

Large gated communities often have both demand and supply for services, yet discovery happens through fragmented WhatsApp groups and word-of-mouth.

Problem

Residents struggle to find nearby services reliably. Service providers lack consistent visibility despite being within the same community.

Solution

A community-scoped platform where residents can discover, book, and order services from verified local providers.

MVP Features

  • Community-restricted service listings
  • Order placement and appointment booking
  • Provider profiles with service details

Success Metrics

  • Service discovery rate
  • Repeat bookings
  • Active providers per community

This case study helped me understand trade-offs between usability, feasibility, and scope — and reinforced the importance of starting small.
